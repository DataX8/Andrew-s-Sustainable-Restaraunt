About Me

I am a 10 year old student in the Fourth Grade. My main passion is in the technology industry, and I love creating an abundance of different things. I am very elated to present to you my project!

About the Project

The goal of the project is to craft an aesthectically appealing and also sustainable building to add to the grand result:"Forthtopia". I was tasked to creating a restaraunt, and equipped it with solar panels, power generating wind tubines, native plants, power walls, locally sourced food and more! I hope you enjoy this project!

Fun Facts

On the top of my 2 feet tall sign, I attached the power generating wind turbine, which is made up of a recycled battery and motor pack, pieces of an old pasta box, zip ties, and tape. This was designed to spin clockwise. I did this by reversing the polarity of the wires, causing the motor to spin backwards, and making the turbine look both aesthectically appealing and a wonderous desingn.
